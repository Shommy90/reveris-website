vision

Reveris is creating an environment that embraces esports, gaming & technology. We discover talent, link disruptive ideas, and empower startups. We bring entrepreneurship to a passionate and creative industry, generating opportunities for business and innovation.

accelerator

A business accelerator that will take ambitious ideas and talented individuals from the community and grow them into startups. And a team of specialists operating as a startup studio to prototype promising ideas from within our ranks and spinning them off.

academy

Our educational program is composed of lectures, workshops and hackathons focused on development of professionals in the esport and gaming industry covering topics about technology and also business development.

entertainment

The Reveris Esports Hub functions as a main venue for our entertainment programs. Here viewing parties and other social activities take place in a cozy esports environment equipped with TV’s, projectors and bar offers.

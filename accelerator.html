The Reveris accelerator

Will feature mentors, our own and from our partners, that will establish a program for helping entrepreneurs with the idea and ambition to make it a real product and further establish a viable business in the esport, gaming industries and related technologies.

Discover talent

We wish to attract ambitious individuals from the esports community through our professional hub programs as hackathons. In this way we will help to unlock the potential of the disruptive esports community and create new products and ideas deriving from it.

Partner collaboration

The accelerator program will be interacting with our esports, business and technology partners for execution support, advise or outsource. Featuring back offices of investors, mentors and partners, where they will follow and assist startups.

Investor attraction

Our accelerator will allow investors for a secure and confident investment in esports, gaming industries and related technologies. Product pitch days will be held for entrepreneurs to present their products and investors to explore the pool of possible startups to invest into.

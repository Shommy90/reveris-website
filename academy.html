We want individuals in the esports and gaming industry to reach their full potential. The Reveris Academy will produce educational content as lectures and workshops covering a variety of topics dedicated to individuals who wish to grow as industry professionals:

  • ● Esports topics will focus on players, coaches and other team members on how to train better, lead a team of players and manage a sport association, etc..
  • ● Business topics will cower startup fundamentals, financing, product research and development, resource gathering etc.
  • ● Finally an array of technology topics connected to esports will be presented as virtual reality, artificial intelligence, blockchain and other related technology.

We plan for our partners to provide experts in fields of interest and prepare and conduct lectures and workshops. This will give our partners also an opportunity to present their company and form connections to the esports community talent pool.
